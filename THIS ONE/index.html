It's harvest season on the apple farm, and you need to make sure you have all the fruit for the winter!

Catch at least 20 ripe Apples in the basket & avoid the rotten ones, as well as other fruit! Or else you'll be hungry for the winter!

CONTROLS:

Move using "A" and "D" keys, or ⬅ & ➡ keys


POINTS:
  • Apples: +1
  • Rotten Apples: -2
  • Non-Apple fruit: -3
